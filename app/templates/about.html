What is this?

The idea of this platform was to look at paticular items, it can be finacial assets, a product you are intersted in buying, allowing people to comment on the pros and cons, include attachements that could be valuable in the desision making process.

This was made with Flask, amazon s3 for image storage, elasticsearch for searching, and postgres for the database.

I based a lot of this project on the fantastic tutorial by Miguel Grinberg which you can find here.
